Our Story

Since opening in 2020, after the world endured the biggest pandemic of the millenium, we wanted to shake up the fresh fruit & vegetable produce industry, so that we can ensure our customers will get great quality goods whilst also knowing the origins. We pride ourselves on the growth of our produce, making sure everything is locally grown in the UK without using any enhances.
With all this in mind, Organic Farm was born to serve the nation with high quality fruits and vegetables.

Why Organic Farm?

Flexible deliveries

Now you can get your deliveries on your terms. Gone are the days spent looking out the window waiting for your food delivery - choose your time and date, and even keep up-to-date in real-time of where your delivery is on our innovative Organic Farm App!

Top quality products

Growing good and honest food locally to the big city so we can bring the fresh countryside flavour to your home. We are a trusted supplier for top restaurants and cruise lines across Britain, and now you can have our premium products in the comfort of your own home.

Vegan, organic and eco-friendly

At Organic Farm being environmentally conscious is at the heart of the organisation. We are constantly trying to reduce our carbon footprint to play our part in the evergrowing climate change crisis. Our products are completely vegan and transported in a most eco-friendly way.
